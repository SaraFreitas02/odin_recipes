Brownies

★★★★★ X reviews

This brownie recipe is a rich and fudgy treat, perfect for satisfying your chocolate cravings. It features a dense and moist texture with a deep chocolate flavor. The combination of cocoa powder and butter creates a decadent dessert that is sure to please any chocolate lover. Enjoy these brownies on their own or with a scoop of ice cream for an extra indulgent experience.
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour an 8 inch square pan.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter. Remove from heat, and stir in 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, and 1 tsp vanilla.
  3. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp baking powder. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 25-30 min. Do not overcook.
