Pancakes

These pancakes are light, fluffy, and perfect for a quick breakfast or brunch. Serve them with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh berries, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Create a well in the center and add milk, egg, and oil. Stir until the mixture is smooth.
  2. Preheat a lightly greased griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or ladle the batter onto the griddle, using about 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until golden brown on both sides and serve warm.
